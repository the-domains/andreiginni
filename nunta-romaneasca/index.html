We would like to invite you to join us on our Romanian wedding party, on the 7th July, in the Carpați (Carpathian in Romanian) mountains. The venue is situated approx. 30’ drive from Sibiu. We will go there around midday and party the whole day and night (of course we will spend the night over).

Meanwhile, since you are in Sibiu, Romania, might as well enjoy this wonderful place!

Sibiu was cultural capital of Europe in 2007 mostly due to its history and surroundings. The best place to be is basically in the city center. You will have there a lot of bars and places to eat. My favourite place would be the Little Square. Also I would encourage you to just stroll around, you wouldn’t need more than 2h to discover little alleys and really nice old houses (the churches, the old city walls, the oldest house in Sibiu - with a gothic facade, the liar’s bridge, pasajul scarilor, parcul cetatii and the towers and many more). And if you have time, I would encourage you to visit the places next to Sibiu such as the Transfagarasan road, the village museum or the mountains … they are gorgeous. Probably the best idea would be to rent a car and go around, the distances are not that big.
